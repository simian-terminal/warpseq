Device

For Warp to be able to send MIDI notes, it needs a way to send them. A device is any computer operating system visible MIDI Device, such as your USB MIDI interface or a virtual one like an Apple IAC Audio Bus.

  In OS X, creating an IAC Bus (see Installation) will allow Warpseq to talk to software synths.

Warp is targeted just as much at hardware synths as software synths, and can speak to multiple devices and instruments at once - including a mix of hardware and software.

  When you start up a new Song, all the current MIDI Devices on your computer should be added to the Song automatically, so you can use them. If you open someone else's Song file, you may need to manually add some new devices and then reconfigure your Instrument definitions in order to play the song on your software and hardware.

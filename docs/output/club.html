Club

Many great resources for Warp users are available on Patreon.

Warp club members have access to extra blog posts, feature polls, warp song files (for every song Michael makes with Warp), our discord channel (community text chat!), extra video livestreams (possibly including some community performances later), and scheduled video chats. This is the inside track to all things Warp and a great way to meet and interact with other users. You may also get some random synth/eurorack-related posts.
